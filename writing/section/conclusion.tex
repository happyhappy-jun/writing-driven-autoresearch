\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

Plain skipping assumes a skipped block's residual update is zero. It is not: much of it is
predictable, and \mname recovers a substantial share of the likelihood skipping destroys, in
closed form, at 0.64\% of prefill latency. Downstream, significant gains appear only where the
damage is severe. Repair pays off where pruning bites (\Cref{sec:app-qwen3}).
